About Me

My name is Emilio Nunez. I am currently a junior student at Elon University, majoring in Journalism and minoring in Film History. I recently just transferred from Georgia State University in Atlanta, where I spent my first two years in College. I have always been interested in photography and spent a week on the Penn State campus furthering that interest and learning about multimedia journalism. Seminars on story selection, newswriting and using photos to enhance storytelling seeded an interest in journalism beyond strictly working with images.
I have always had a keen interest in the past, and one magazine in particular resonated with me like no other: TIME magazine's "100 Greatest Images: History's Most Influential Photographs." Published in 2012, it showcased photos beginning with the first picture ever taken in 1826 all the way through the modern era. I hope to blaze my own trail in this long journalism tradition.
